About Me

I am a doctoral researcher at Université de Technologie de Compiègne (UTC), LMAC Laboratory. My research integrates topology optimization and PDE analysis with AI-driven modeling for thermal energy systems, including phase change materials (PCM) and Kapitza interface laws.

Previously, I obtained my Master's degree in Computational Mathematics from Northwestern Polytechnical University (Double First-Class/985 University), under the supervision of Academician Weihong Zhang and Prof. Piotr Breitkopf. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from the same institution.
